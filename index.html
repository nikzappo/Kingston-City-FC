Kingston City FC

Established in 1974, KCFC is an Australian, Semi-Professional Soccer Club from Clayton South, Victoria.

About Kingston FC

  • League

    Currently playing in Victorian NPL2.

  • Community Club

    We have teams from under 7s to Over 35s.

  • Rich in History

    Over 40 years of Football Participation in the Vic State Leagues.

  • Stadium

    We currently play at the Grange Reserve.
